Digital Art for Beginners

SATURDAYS 10:30am - 12:30pm • May 26 - June 23, 2018

This 5-week course will introduce students to the basics of working with digital illustration software. The course covers tools and layers, drawing with a pen tablet, best practices for file setup, and the step-by-step of creating a digital illustration. Students will be able to create a character illustration digitally from start to finish by the end of the course. Students must provide their own equipment.
