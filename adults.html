With the exception of private lessons, up to 6 students can enroll in each course.

☆ NEW - Digital Art for Beginners ☆ for older teens & adults

SATURDAYS 3pm - 5pm • May 26 - June 30, 2018 • $195

Do you want to learn to draw and paint digitally? This course will introduce students to the basics of working with digital illustration software as an industry professional. Students will learn the workflow and best practices of illustrating digitally.

Course info

☆ NEW - Drawing with Colour ☆

WEDNESDAYS 7pm - 9pm • May 23 - June 27, 2018 • $225

This 6-week course focuses on strengthening the fundamentals of observational drawing. Students are encouraged to think about their subjects in colour, and expand their use of media. We will use washes of paint and ink to add colour and energy to our pieces, and break away from traditional pencil sketching.
