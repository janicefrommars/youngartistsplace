Art classes for kids

I offer general drawing & painting classes for kids aged 6-11. Students of all abilities are welcome!

My classes often make use of stories, science, and connections to our real world to help children learn about art. I focus on teaching practical art techniques, and encourage children to be independent and imaginative in their art-making. We look at both historic and contemporary artists to learn art vocabulary and techniques.
