Digital Art for Beginners

Students will learn how to use professional drawing and painting software to create illustrations on the computer from start-to-finish. Perfect for students who are interested in anime, illustration, or comics, and who already have a good grasp of drawing fundamentals. We'll hone our drawing skills during the lessons as well, so that you not only learn how to use the digital tools, but also how to use colour and form to create a solid work of art.

No prior experience with digital art is needed. All styles of expression are respected and welcome here.

I recommend using Paint Tool SAI to learn the basics of digital art. It's a more intuitive (and more affordable) program than Photoshop, but many of the principles we learn will transfer over to Photoshop, as well as most other drawing and painting programs. The studio is equipped with a large screen computer, and the newest pen tablet from Wacom. Students may also bring in their own laptops and equipment if they prefer their own setup.
