Art & comic classes for creative kids

Young Artists' Place is a small studio in Burnaby, BC that offers art classes focusing on drawing and storytelling. We work in mediums like digital art and comics, while also emphasizing fundamental drawing skills through step-by-step demonstration of tools and techniques. Check out our class offerings, or read more about the studio's approach on the "About" page!
