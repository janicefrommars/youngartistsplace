Classes for teens

If you've never taken a class with me before, I invite you to come to the studio for a free trial class! This free class is 1 hour long and all supplies are provided.

Digital Art

Students will learn how to use professional drawing and painting software to create illustrations on the computer from start-to-finish. Perfect for students who are interested in anime, illustration, or comics, and who already have a good grasp of drawing fundamentals. We'll hone our drawing skills during the lessons as well, so that you not only learn how to use the digital tools, but also how to use colour and form to create a solid work of art.

No prior experience with digital art is needed. Beginners and intermediate digital artists are welcome, and all styles of expression are respected and welcome here, whether you're an anime artist, a cartoonist, or a digital painter.

I recommend using Paint Tool SAI to learn the basics of digital art. It's a more intuitive (and more affordable) program than Photoshop, but many of the principles we learn will transfer over to Photoshop, as well as most other drawing and painting programs.

All equipment is provided. The studio is equipped with a large screen computer, and the newest pen tablet from Wacom. Students may also bring in their own laptops and equipment if they prefer their own setup.

Drawing

A general drawing and painting class that focuses specifically on 2D mediums. This class helps students explore subject matter that they are personally interested in, while exposing them to different types of mediums and techniques that they may not have access to on their own. We'll use inks, paints, and a variety of tools to get away from just regular pencil drawing.

Each class experience is different - I listen to what each student is interested in, and structure lesson plans around that. My goal is to help each student obtain confidence and the feeling of mastery over a variety of art tools and techniques, so that they can then use these tools to express their own artistic voice.
