Ink & Watercolour

In this class, students learn about the unique properties of ink and watercolour, how they interact with water and paper, and develop fine motor control over brushes and other lining tools. We'll go through the process of creating finished watercolour paintings, from sketch to completion. Depending on student interest, topics covered may include illustration, portraits, the human figure, animals, and still life.

Drawing

Drawing doesn't have to be just black-and-white pencil sketching! In this class, drawing techniques and principles are taught through varied and interesting mediums and methods. Students will have the opportunity to draw and experiment with different types of professional artist materials, such as pastels, pencil crayons, ink, and graphite. We will observe and draw from real life objects or photographs, and I encourage students to think actively about problem-solving and decision-making through drawing, rather than simply copying the reference material.
