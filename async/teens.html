Fall 2018 Schedule

☆ NEW - Drawing Manga Characters ☆ 9-13yrs

SATURDAYS 1pm-2pm • Nov 17 - Dec 15, 2018 (5 weeks) • $95

Create your own manga characters in this 5-week class! Students will learn drawing skills, including proportion, anatomy, and expressions, in an anime/manga style. We'll go over the step-by-step of drawing faces, hair, and clothing, and students will create colour illustrations of their own character designs.

Inked Comics 12-16yrs

SATURDAYS 10:30am - 12pm • Nov 17 - Dec 15, 2018 (5 weeks) • $140

Do you want to learn to bring your comics from rough pencils to polished inks? In this 5-week course, teens will learn about the unique properties of ink, and practice techniques for using brushes and nibs to create bold, crisp artwork for comics.

Course info

Illustrative Painting 13-17yrs

SUNDAYS 2:30pm - 4pm • Sep 9 - Nov 4, 2018 (8 weeks, no class Oct 7) • $225

This 8-week course covers both watercolour and gouache painting. Looking at both historic and contemporary illustrators for inspiration, teens will learn to bring their drawings to life with a better understanding of colour, contrast, and form.
