☆ Comics Summer Camp for Teens ☆ 12-16yrs

July 23 - 27, 2018 • 9:30am - 12:30pm • $225

In this one-week camp for teens, we'll create our own original comic stories. Students will learn about scripting an engaging story, thumbnailing and page layout, drawing characters, and lettering. Beginners and intermediate cartoonists are all welcome!

Digital Art for Beginners 12-19yrs

SATURDAYS 10:30am - 12pm • May 26 - June 23, 2018 • $125

Do you want to learn to draw and paint digitally? This 5-week course will introduce students to the basics of working with digital illustration software. Students will be able to create a character or portrait illustration digitally from start to finish by the end of the course.

Course info

Character Design for Comics 12-19 yrs

SUNDAYS 10:30am - 12pm • May 27 - June 24, 2018 • $145

A vibrant, well-designed cast of characters can make a huge difference in getting an audience invested in a story. In this 5-week course, I'll show teens my process for how to design characters for the comics and cartooning medium.

Course info

Digital Art for Beginners ☆ for older teens & adults

SATURDAYS 3pm - 5pm • May 26 - June 30, 2018 • $195

Are you considering a career in the arts? This 6-week course covers the basics of working with digital illustration software as an industry professional. Students will learn the workflow and best practices of illustrating digitally.
