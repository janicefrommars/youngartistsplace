Comics Summer Camp for Teens

This one-week art camp runs for 3 hours every day, Monday to Friday from July 23 - 27, 2018.

Over the course of the week, we'll create our own original comic stories. Students will learn about the process of creating a comic, including how to write a script for an engaging story, as well as best practices in comics for page layout and lettering. We'll devote some time to strengthening drawing fundamentals as well, in the context of drawing characters for comics. At the end of the camp, we'll publish the class' work online so everyone can share and admire their stories.

Beginners and intermediate cartoonists are all welcome! All supplies are included.

---

About the instructor: Janice Liu is an illustrator and comic artist, in addition to being the owner-operator of Young Artists' Place. Her comics have been published in teen & literary magazines, and in Wayward Sisters anthology by TO Comix Press. She is also the artist and co-creator of the webcomic Chicken Soup & Goji Berries 中药鸡汤. Janice has been teaching art for kids since 2013, and recently worked with Burnaby Public Library to deliver a teens' comic workshop.
