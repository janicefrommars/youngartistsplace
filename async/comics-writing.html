Writing & Drawing Comics

Get notified of new classes

An intermediate comic class for students who’ve completed my Young Cartoonists class. Students will continue to build their skills in comic creation by delving more in-depth into both the writing and drawing aspects of the comic craft.

Topics will vary with each session, but may include writing for different genres of comics (narrative, non-fiction, autobiography, poetry), drawing characters and backgrounds, and anatomy and perspective.

Students new to comics should enroll in this intro class first: Young Cartoonists

---

About the instructor: Janice Liu is an illustrator and comic artist, in addition to being the owner-operator of Young Artists' Place. Her comics have been published in teen & literary magazines, and in Wayward Sisters anthology by TO Comix Press. She is also the artist and co-creator of the webcomic Chicken Soup & Goji Berries 中药鸡汤. Janice has been teaching art for kids since 2013, and works with schools and libraries to deliver comic workshops.
