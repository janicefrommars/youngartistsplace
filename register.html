Young Artists' Place
art classes for children and teens | Burnaby, BC

Book a class time that fits your schedule!


To register, please email me (janice@youngartistsplace.com) or call me (604-780-2283) with your preferred times for class. I am very flexible with scheduling, and all efforts will be made to accomodate students' schedules and availability!

I provide students with paper and canvases, but the cost of other supplies is not included in the class prices. Brushes, palettes, and paints generally cost around $25-35 in total depending on the class. Supplies can be purchased from the studio or on your own - you can ask me for a supply list if you'd like to buy them yourself.
