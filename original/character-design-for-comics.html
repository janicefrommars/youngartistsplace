Character Design for Comics

Have you ever wanted to learn how to design a cast of compelling characters for your comics?

How do you make characters look consistent every time you draw them? How can you use references to make your designs more believable while still sticking to your own visual style?

Because this course makes heavy use of research and references, students must bring an electronic device with which they can access Wi-fi (smartphones, tablets, iPads, and laptops are all ok). Please remember to charge the device before classes.
