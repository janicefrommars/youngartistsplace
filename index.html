Art classes for the young-at-heart

Young Artists' Place is a small studio in Burnaby, BC that offers personalized art classes focusing on practical drawing and painting skills. The studio takes a modern approach by offering instruction in new and contemporary mediums, like digital art, cartooning, and comics. A new generation of young artists is emerging, and art doesn't always have to be hanging in a gallery anymore!
