Private art classes to suit your needs

Young Artists' Place is a small studio in Burnaby, BC that offers personalized private and semi-private art classes. Whether you're an aspiring anime artist, a student with special needs, or a hobbyist who wants to dive into digital art, classes here are tailored to your specific needs.

The studio takes a modern approach by offering instruction in new and contemporary mediums, like digital art and comics. A new generation of young artists is emerging, and art doesn't always have to be hanging in a gallery anymore!

Learn More

Drawing & Painting

Personalized drawing and painting classes that focus specifically on 2D mediums and the fundamentals of drawing. We'll use inks, paints, and a variety of tools to get away from just regular paper and pencil.

Digital Art

Ideal for teens or adults who are interested in illustration, anime, or comics, and who already have a good grasp of drawing fundamentals. No prior experience needed!
